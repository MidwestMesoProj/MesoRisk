NOAA severe-weather probability methodology
MesoMax RiskSim is an experimental environment model. The conditional probability functions in this single-file build are not operational SPC probabilities and are not calibrated to an official SPC verification dataset. To create genuinely fitted distributions, train the conditional surfaces against historical sounding/mesoanalysis and LSR data.
